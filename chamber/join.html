NP Membership - Non-Profit

Cost: No Fee

Perfect for non-profit organizations committed to community service. This membership provides access to networking events, community resources, and chamber advocacy efforts.

Benefits Include:

  • Access to monthly networking events
  • Community resource directory
  • Chamber advocacy and support
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Basic member directory listing
×

Bronze Membership

Cost: $150/year

Essential membership for small businesses and startups looking to establish their presence in the community.

Benefits Include:

  • All NP membership benefits
  • Business directory listing with logo
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Access to business workshops
  • Ribbon cutting ceremony
  • Basic website promotion
×

Silver Membership

Cost: $300/year

Enhanced membership for growing businesses seeking greater visibility and networking opportunities.

Benefits Include:

  • All Bronze membership benefits
  • Priority event registration
  • Social media promotion
  • Quarterly business spotlight
  • Leadership development workshops
  • Enhanced directory features
  • Business referral network access
×

Gold Membership

Cost: $500/year

Premium membership for established businesses that want maximum visibility and influence in the chamber.

Benefits Include:

  • All Silver membership benefits
  • Featured homepage spotlight
  • Board of directors voting rights
  • Annual business profile feature
  • Exclusive executive networking events
  • Complimentary event sponsorship opportunities
  • Priority support and advocacy
